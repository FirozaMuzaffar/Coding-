Burger Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Fresh bread buns
  • Beef patty
  • Lettuce, tomatoes, onions
  • Special sauce
  • Cheese

Instructions:

Cook the beef patty to your desired level of doneness. Toast the buns and spread the special sauce on each side. Assemble the burger by layering lettuce, tomatoes, onions, and cheese on the bottom bun, then add the cooked patty. Top with the other bun and serve with fries.
